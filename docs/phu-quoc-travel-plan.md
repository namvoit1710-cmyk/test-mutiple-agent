# Traveling Plan to Phu Quoc

## Overview
A relaxing getaway to the pearl island of Vietnam.

## Day 1: Arrival and Beach Relaxation
- Morning: Arrive at Phu Quoc International Airport.
- Afternoon: Check into resort and relax at Long Beach.
- Evening: Seafood dinner at Duong Dong Night Market.

## Day 2: North Island Exploration
- Morning: Visit Ganh Dau Cape for views of Cambodia.
- Afternoon: Explore VinWonders or Safari.
- Evening: Visit Grand World Phu Quoc.

## Day 3: Island Hopping and Snorkeling
- Morning: Speedboat trip to surrounding small islands.
- Afternoon: Snorkeling and swimming in crystal clear waters.
- Evening: Sunset cocktail at a beach bar.

## Day 4: Culture and Nature
- Morning: Visit Phu Quoc Prison and Pepper Farm.
- Afternoon: Explore the Fish Sauce factory.
- Evening: Farewell dinner and packing.

## Day 5: Departure
- Morning: Last stroll on the beach.
- Afternoon: Depart from airport.
